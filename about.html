About Us

PT. Protonema was established on July 3, 2013. Protonema was founded by Mr. Mochamad Taat Baryanto who also served as the Company's President Director. Protonema is enganged in IT Services company focusing on Business Consultancy, Manage Operation, and Software Development.

Vision

To be a leading IT company that is competitive by providing leading services in producing IT products and services as the best, integrated, professionally managed solution and prioritizing customer satisfaction for sustainable relationships.

Mission

  • Provide the best service for achieving customer satisfaction with guaranteed quality of work, efficiency, directed speed, accuracy and competitive prices.
  • Promoting professionalism and teamwork in producing quality services.
  • Improve capabilities by optimizing the management and development of superior and trustworthy and reliable human resources.
  • Develop mutually beneficial business cooperation and partnerships.
  • Optimizing the best and most recent technological innovation in each product.
